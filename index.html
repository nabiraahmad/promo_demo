ABOUT

Tyler the Creator is an American rapper and record producer. He is one of the founding members of the music collective Odd Future. Okonma has won two Grammy Awards, three BET Hip Hop Awards, a BRIT Award, and a MTV Video Music Award. Some of his top albums are IGOR, Goblin, CALL ME IF YOU GET LOST, and Flower Boy.
